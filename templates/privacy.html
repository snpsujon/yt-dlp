1. Overview

At Universal Media Downloader, we respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it. By using our Chrome extension, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No browsing history tracking
  • No user-identifiable data
  • No data sharing with third parties

3. Permissions

The extension may request the following:

  • ActiveTab – to allow media downloads from the current page
  • Clipboard Access – to paste links from your clipboard
  • Storage – to save preferences like dark mode or format

These are used only to support functionality. No data is sent externally.

4. Data Handling

All download requests are processed securely. We do not log or store any media URLs or downloaded content.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party trackers.

6. Children’s Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.
